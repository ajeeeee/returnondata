Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

What is an E-Commerce Website or Online Store?

Just like in the physical world there are "Brick and Mortar" stores where people interact in-person, an online store or E-Commerce store is a place to buy and sell on the internet by means of a web browser. Where you can interact with the shop owner, purchase products, track delivery to your home or office, and provide feedback in reviews about your purchases and experiences.

What can I sell on my store?

Normally, online stores provide both digital and physical products and provide the facility to download digital purchases and or a virtual order to a physical operation for physical products to be shipped to you.

What are some of the advantages of an E-Commerce Store?

  • Global or local access to products where you can find products and services that suit your needs fast and easy with your pc, mac, laptop, tablet or mobile phone.

  • 24 Hours, 7 Days a Week, 365 Calendar a Year access to a Brand of products from their official company website that provides authentic company inventory to be shipped to you.

  • Owner dashboards since the store operates on two fronts, E-Commerce store would offer 2 dashboards for the effective management of both; the back end and the front end. Get an overview of total products, sales, and orders along with a list of your top and recent orders here. Also, find the order graph of your past 15 days here. Get entire store analytics, right from traffic through various browsers, devices, and platforms to your top URLs, get every insight about your visitors on this dashboard.

  • Customer login and registration with the help of the customer login and registration module, the customer can only place the order when he/ she has already registered and logged in. This is for the safety and security purposes of the customer as well as the owner due to the authentication of information. Also, the customer can take a look at his/ her previous orders.

Do most E-Commerce stores look the same?

In general, there is a certain acceptable layout which provides an experience to buyers online similar to a brick and mortar store. The E-Commerce store has sections for selling products in a category, or by price, or by seasonality, or by discounted sales, the object is to merchandise your products so everyone knows what is hot and not, and a feeling of dynamic buying for shoppers.

How do E-Commerce stores handle payments online and offline?

Our online E-Commerce store supports multiple payment methods to deal with online store purchases and transactions by providing online payment processing for Stripe, PayPal, Paystack, Flutterwave, Razorpay, Paytm, Mollie, Skrill, Mercado Pago, CoinGate, Paymentwall.

Also support off-line payments by tracking direct to bank deposit and C.O.D. as the most popular ways of offline payments.
